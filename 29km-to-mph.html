29 km to mph

To convert 29 kilometers per hour to miles per hour, Multiply 29 km/h by 0.621371. 29 km/h times 0.621371 gives you 18.02 mph. Therefore, a speed of 29 km/h is equivalent to 18.02 mph.

Formula for 29 km/h to mph

Given a speed of 29 km/h, the conversion to miles per hour (mph) can be expressed as:

v = 0.621371 * u

Where:

  • v is the speed in mph,
  • u is the speed in km/h.

Substituting the given speed into the formula:

v = 0.621371 * 29 km/h

Therefore,

v = 18.02 mph
