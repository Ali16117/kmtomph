62 km to mph

To convert 62 kilometers per hour to miles per hour, Multiply 62 km/h by 0.621371. 62 km/h times 0.621371 gives you 38.53 mph. Therefore, a speed of 62 km/h is equivalent to 38.53 mph.

Formula for 62 km/h to mph

Given a speed of 62 km/h, the conversion to miles per hour (mph) can be expressed as:

v = 0.621371 * u

Where:

  • v is the speed in mph,
  • u is the speed in km/h.

Substituting the given speed into the formula:

v = 0.621371 * 62 km/h

Therefore,

v = 38.53 mph
