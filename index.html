Km to mph

Kilometers per hour (km/h) and miles per hour (mph) are both units of speed. The kilometer per hour is a unit of speed expressing the number of kilometers traveled in one hour. It is commonly used worldwide except in the United States and the United Kingdom, where miles per hour is the standard.

Miles per hour, on the other hand, is an imperial unit of speed expressing the number of statute miles covered in one hour. Despite the UK's official metrication program, miles per hour are still used there for road speed limits and road signs.

Conversion from km/h to mph

To convert speed from kilometers per hour to miles per hour, you need to use the formula:

mph = 0.621371 * v

where v is the speed in km/h and 0.621371 is the conversion factor from km/h to mph.

Therefore, to convert 1 km/h to mph, you can multiply 1 by 0.621371 to get approximately 0.621371 mph.
